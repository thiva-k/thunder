# Customer Identity for B2C Applications

ThunderID enables seamless customer identity for B2C apps—fast onboarding, simple sign-in, and secure recovery across web and mobile. This page highlights what’s possible for consumer products, now and in the future.

## What B2C Needs

<ul className="uc-checklist">
	<li>Support social, password, and passwordless sign-in.</li>
	<li>Keep registration short and collect profile data progressively.</li>
	<li>Add step-up authentication only for high-risk actions.</li>
	<li>Provide reliable account recovery paths.</li>
</ul>

## Example Customer Journey

<ol className="uc-stage-list">
	<li><strong>Start registration:</strong> use email or social sign-up.</li>
	<li><strong>Introduce passkeys:</strong> register passkeys during onboarding or after first sign-in.</li>
	<li><strong>Prefer passkeys:</strong> keep password or magic link as fallback.</li>
	<li><strong>Step up only when needed:</strong> challenge sensitive operations with stronger verification.</li>
	<li><strong>Close the loop:</strong> provide secure recovery through verified email channels.</li>
</ol>
